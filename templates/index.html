Moonwalker

How did the master of the moon walk end up in this dark place and what's there to find? To escape, find the door and the key to unlock it.

Play
How to play
Use the arrow keys of your keyboard to navigate through the dark dungeon.(if you find it too hard, you can hold 'f') Use the spacebar to shoot your arrow and kill the creature that may hurt you. (Keep in mind only one arrow can be shot at a time)
